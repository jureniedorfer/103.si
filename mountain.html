Approach

A tailored approach.

A successful mountain day begins well before the trailhead. It begins with planning, honest expectations, and a route that matches the guest’s physical ability, confidence, ambition, and reason for being there.

103 Mountain is intentionally personal and selective. It is not a standard guiding offer built around generic itineraries, fixed packages, or a one size fits all idea of the mountains.

Safety comes first. Good decisions create the space for a day that can feel more relaxed, more present, and more alive: steady movement, good conversations, quiet moments, changing views, and the simple pleasure of being fully outside.

What I offer

Experiences shaped around fit.

  • Private mountain experiences for individuals and small groups
  • Route selection based on real ability, ambition, and conditions
  • A safety-led and judgment-based approach
  • A mountain adventure shaped by presence and care
  • A more personal and attentive guiding format
  • Experiences built around pace and quality rather than volume

How I guide

Attention, calmness, and responsibility.

The goal is not to push people through a generic experience, but to create a day in the mountains that feels coherent from start to finish.

That usually means good preparation, honest communication, steady pacing, and the willingness to adapt when conditions or people require it. Some challenge is part of the experience, but it has to be the right challenge.
