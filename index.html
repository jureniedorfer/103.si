What 103 is

A personal practice, not a broad service business.

The work is intentionally personal. Film projects are approached with technical precision, field awareness, and clear execution. Mountain days are shaped around safety, trust, good decisions, honest conversation, and the simple pleasure of being fully outside.

It is intentionally selective. Not because it is distant, but because good work needs attention. The best collaborations and the best mountain days do not come from volume, fixed packages, or broad appeal. They come from the right fit, careful preparation, and enough space for the experience to unfold properly.
