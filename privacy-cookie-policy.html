Activity Documentation

Data related to the performance of activities may be stored for as long as necessary for:

Data related to the performance of activities, complaints, insurance procedures, or possible claims may be stored until expiry of the periods within which legal claims may be asserted or defended, or until completion of the relevant procedure if it lasts longer.

Data Processed on the Basis of Consent

Data processed on the basis of consent is stored until withdrawal of consent or until fulfillment of the purpose for which consent was given, unless another appropriate legal basis exists for further storage.

Technical and Analytical Data

Technical and analytical website data is stored in accordance with the settings of the individual tool or cookie, as stated in the Cookie Policy or cookie settings.

Erasure, Destruction, or Anonymization

After expiry of the retention period, personal data is erased, destroyed, or anonymized, unless further storage is required by applicable legislation or is necessary for the assertion, exercise, or defense of legal claims.
