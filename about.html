About Jure Niedorfer

I work through 103, my personal practice built around two distinct fields: film work in demanding terrain and tailored mountain experiences for private guests.

Judgment, movement, accountability.

My professional background has been shaped over more than twenty years in film. I work as a cinematographer and producer, with a particular strength in mountain environments and other difficult-access settings.

The mountains have also been a constant in my life far beyond film. I have been active in climbing, alpinism, and mountaineering since 1995, and my academic background in geography has shaped how I think and work: with preparation, patience, restraint, and respect for consequences.

What I value most in the mountains is not only movement, achievement, or reaching the top. It is the quality of the day: good decisions, honest pace, attention to people, open conversation, quiet moments, and the feeling that life becomes a little simpler outside.

I prefer a limited number of thoughtful, well-matched collaborations and private mountain days over a high volume of work. That allows me to stay close to the details, maintain a high standard, and shape each production or mountain experience with the level of attention it deserves.
