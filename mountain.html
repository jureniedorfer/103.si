Approach

A tailored approach.

A successful mountain day begins well before the trailhead. It begins with planning, honest expectations, and a route that matches the guest’s physical ability, confidence, ambition, and sense of what they are actually looking for.

103 Mountain is intentionally personal and selective. It is not a standard guiding offer built around generic itineraries.

Safety comes first. I do not place achievement above sound judgment, and guests must be willing to respect decisions related to terrain, pace, route changes, and conditions.

What I offer

Experiences shaped around fit.

  • Private mountain experiences for individuals and small groups
  • Route selection based on real ability, ambition, and conditions
  • Careful planning before the day begins
  • A safety-led and judgment-based approach
  • A more personal and attentive guiding format
  • Experiences shaped around pace and quality rather than volume

How I guide

Attention, calmness, and responsibility.

The goal is not to push people through a generic experience, but to create a day in the mountains that feels coherent from start to finish.

That usually means good preparation, honest communication, steady pacing, and the willingness to adapt when conditions or people require it. Some challenge is part of the experience, but it has to be the right challenge.
